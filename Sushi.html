Sushi Rolls Recipe

Description

Sushi is a Japanese dish featuring specially prepared rice and usually some type of fish or seafood, often raw, but sometimes cooked.

.

Ingredients

  • 1 ⅓ cups water
  • ⅔ cup uncooked short-grain white rice
  • 3 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 4 sheets nori seaweed sheets
  • ½ pound imitation crabmeat, flaked
  • ½ cucumber, peeled, cut into small strips
  • 2 tablespoons pickled ginger
  • 1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and sliced

    • Steps

    1. Preheat the oven to 300 degrees F
    2. Bring water to a boil in a medium pot; stir in rice
    3. Mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt in a small bowl
    4. Mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt in a small bowl
    5. Heat nori in the preheated oven until warm, 1 to 2 minutes
    6. Spread a thin layer of rice on top.
    7. Arrange 1/4 of the crabmeat, avocado, cucumber, and pickled ginger over rice
    8. Make rolls and cut them into pieces
