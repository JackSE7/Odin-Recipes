Lasagna Recipe

Description

lasagna has to be one of the most loved comfort foods in the world. Hailing from Italy before making its way through the world and taking on many different forms, lasagna has to be one of the most popular pasta dishes of all time.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef chuck
  • 3 ounces tomato paste (half a 6-ounce cann)
  • 1 (28-ounce)can good-quality tomato sauce
  • 1/2 medium onion, diced (about 3/4 cup)
  • 1/2 large bell pepper (green, red, or yellow), diced (about 3/4 cup)
  • 1 1/2 pounds (24 ounces) mozzarella cheese, grated or sliced
  • 1/2 pound dry lasagna noodles (requires 9 lasagna noodles - unbroken)
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 pound (4 ounces) freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Put pasta water to boil
  2. Brown the ground beef
  3. Cook the bell pepper, onions, and garlic; add back the beef (4-5 mins)
  4. Make the sauce
  5. Boil and drain the lasagna noodles
  6. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F
  7. Assemble the lasagna
